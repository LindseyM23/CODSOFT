Us...

Welcome to Delight Softies Manufacturing Enterprise, where quality meets innovation! Founded in 2018 by Monyepao Thabang, who embarked on this journey at the age of 23, our company is dedicated to bringing delightful, soft, and comfortable products to homes and businesses around the world. Situated in the vibrant city of Polokwane, Limpopo, Delight Softies has grown from a passionate idea into a thriving manufacturing enterprise known for its commitment to excellence.



Our story

At Delight Softies, our journey began with a simple vision: to create soft, comforting products that bring joy and satisfaction to our customers. Inspired by the natural beauty and warmth of Limpopo, Monyepao Thabang set out to build a company that not only offers superior quality but also fosters a sense of community and well-being.




What we specialize in

Delight Softies specializes in crafting an extensive range of soft and cozy products, including plush toys, cushions, blankets, and more. Our manufacturing processes are rooted in innovation and sustainability, ensuring that each product not only meets but exceeds industry standards. We pride ourselves on using high-quality materials and employing skilled artisans who share our passion for delivering excellence.
